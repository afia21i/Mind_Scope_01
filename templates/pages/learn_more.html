About MindScope

MindScope provides free, confidential mental-health self-screenings to help you understand your current well-being and decide whether professional support might be helpful. Our mission is to make evidence-based tools accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

Important: MindScope is an educational resource and is not a substitute for professional evaluation, diagnosis, or treatment. If your screening results suggest significant distress—or if you have concerns about your mental health—please consult a qualified mental-health professional such as a psychiatrist, psychologist, or licensed therapist.

Validated Screening Tools

All questionnaires offered on MindScope are evidence-based and widely used by mental-health professionals. You can review the original sources below:

  • PHQ-9 (Depression) – Official PHQ-9 website
  • GAD-7 (Anxiety) – NCBI publication (Spitzer et al.)
  • Perceived Stress Scale (PSS-10) – Mind Garden / Cohen et al.
